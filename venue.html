Venue

The ICEODIS 2026 conference will take place in the historic city of Taza, Morocco. Taza is known for its breathtaking landscapes, rich cultural heritage, and warm hospitality. The conference venue will provide state-of-the-art facilities for keynote sessions, workshops, and networking events in a professional and engaging environment. Participants will also have the chance to explore Taza's famous caves, scenic mountains, and the old city. The event promises a perfect blend of academic exchange and immersive cultural experiences in the heart of Morocco.
